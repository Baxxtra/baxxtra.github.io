Assistant Professor in Finance

I am a passionate researcher in the field of sustainable finance, biodiversity, and climate finance, and a recipient of the 2024 Lamfalussy Research Fellowship from the European Central Bank (ECB).

​​​​In my research, I explore how investors assess corporate climate risks and how text analysis and AI can enhance the identification of risk profiles. Additionally, I examine investors' reliance on ESG ratings, climate risk scores, and corporate disclosures, as well as the methodologies behind ESG scoring and potential greenwashing risks.
